Welcome to BOPSOC

Previous Next
The Big Open Party Society (or BOPSoc for short) is a collective of cultures across Leeds universities predominantly from Black, Asain or Minority Ethnic communities that wish to collaborate and take part in a cultural exchange in order to reduce ignorance and increase mindfulness on campus. With national and worldwide atrocities such as Brexit and the recent New Zealand terror attack it is more important now than ever that we don’t show hatred towards those from different cultures or creeds and rather create an open and welcoming atmosphere!
The idea for this society stemmed from the 2018 Inspire, Learn Lead BAME Conference where members of our Committee learned about the attainment gap. What is the attainment gap? This is the discrepancy in academic statistics between sixth form and college achievement compared to academic achievement at University. One of the major perks of university is the chance to meet life-long friends and experience a buzzing social life, this is often different for BAME students as they don’t find that all social circles are inclusive. This is where BOPSoc lends a helping hand!
