Meet The Committee



As you can see BOPSOC is a student run society and the committe can be seen in the photos below! They are responsible for putting on cultural events and socials that are designed to strengthen the bonds within the BAME community as well as non BAME students. THe committee are a highly motivated group of students who recognise the injustices that BAME students face. BAME students are 1.5x more likely to drop out of university and less likely to do well in comparison to their non-BAME counterparts. Therefore this society and the committte aim to combat these figures. Please get in touch via the social media links at the bottom of the page if you would like to speak to one of them... or if you would like to join the committee yourself!
